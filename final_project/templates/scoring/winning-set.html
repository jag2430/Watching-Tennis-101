Winning A Set

A player needs to win six (6) games in order to win a set.

If the score gets to 5-5 (5-all), one player must win the next two games to win the set.

If the score reaches 6-6 (6-all) in the set, a tiebreaker is played.

The winner of the tiebreaker wins the set by a score of 7-6.
