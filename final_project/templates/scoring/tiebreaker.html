Tiebreaker

The next person who was due to serve will start the tiebreaker by serving one point to the deuce side of the court.

The following two points will then be served by the opponent starting on the ad side.

Players switch ends of the court every six points played. The first player to win seven points, by two, wins the tiebreaker (e.g. 7-5).

The player who served the first point of the tiebreaker will be receiving to start the next set.
