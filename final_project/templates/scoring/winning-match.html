Winning The Match

The most common format used in tennis is a best-of-three sets format.

However, in Grand Slams, (Australian Open, Roland Garros, Wimbledon, US Open), a best-of-five sets format is used.

When a match reaches 6-6 in the deciding set at a Grand Slam, a 10-point tiebreaker will be played.

The first player to win 10 points, with an advantage of two or more points, wins.
