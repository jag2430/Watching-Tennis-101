Deuce

When the score is 40-40, it is called deuce.

At deuce, the player who wins the next point has advantage (AD).

If the player with the advantage wins the next point, they win the game.

If they lose the point, the score goes back to deuce. A player needs to win two consecutive points after deuce to win a game.
