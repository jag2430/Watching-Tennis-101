Gameplay

A player must hit a shot either before the ball has bounced or after the first bounce. If the ball bounces twice, then the player with the last shot wins the point.

A player must also wait for the ball to pass the net and come over to their side of the court. The player cannot step over the net to hit the ball. The player also cannot touch the net when the point is live.
