Starting The Point

A point begins when the serving player hits a serve into the opponent's service box on the opposite side from where they are standing. The server gets two chances to serve the ball into play. If they miss both, they lose the point. This is known as a double fault.

The serving player alternates between standing to the right of the center mark (deuce side of the court) and to the left (ad side of the court).
